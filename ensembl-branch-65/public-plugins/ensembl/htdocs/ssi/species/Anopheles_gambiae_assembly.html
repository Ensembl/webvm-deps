Please note that from release 56, Anopheles gambiae will no longer be part of the main Ensembl release. Instead the data will be incorporated into our new sister site, Ensembl Metazoa.

Ensembl data and displays for Anopheles gambiae are made possible through a joint effort by the Ensembl group and VectorBase, a NIAID Bioinformatics Resource Center. Please access this genome and other resources via the Vectorbase browser.

Assembly

The genome assembly presented here (AgamP3, February 2006) is a revised assembly based on the whole genome shotgun assembly of the PEST strain of Anopheles gambiae produced by the The International Anopheles Genome Project and described in Holt et al (October 2002) with revisions as described in Sharakhova et al (2007). More details can be found at VectorBase.
