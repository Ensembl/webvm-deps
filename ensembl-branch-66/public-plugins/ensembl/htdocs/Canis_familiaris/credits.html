Dog Genome Sequencing Consortium

The dog genome project is a consortium led by:

  • The Broad Institute - Plasmid and fosmid libraries and sequencing, assembly

The other members of the consortium, in alphabetical order are:

  • Agencourt Bioscience - BAC-end sequencing
  • Centre National de la Recherche Scientifique (CNRS) - RH map
  • Children's Hospital Oakland Research Instituter (CHORI) - BAC libraries
  • Fred Hutchinson Cancer Research Center (FHCRC) - Dog Selection
  • North Carolina State University (NCSU) - FISH mapping
